## About Mountain Fog

Mountain Fog is a boutique consulting firm specializing in cloud computing, big-data analytics, and natural language processing. We particularly enjoy projects that integrate across these domains.

As consulting partners with both AWS and Google Cloud, our team includes certified professional architects and engineers from each platform. We have developed proprietary software tools for cloud resource management and share these with our clients.

We are selective with our clients because we want to ensure we can help make your project a success. We serve startups, corporations of various sizes, and government agencies, adapting our approach to each client's maturity level and processes.
