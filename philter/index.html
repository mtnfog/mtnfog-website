Redact PHI and PII from text and documents

Philter redacts PII and PHI from documents to help you maintain HIPAA compliance, meet industry regulations, and leverage your documents for valuable secondary purposes.

Philter features a redaction API that identifies and redacts PHI and PII from text and documents. Philter runs entirely in your own cloud. Your data never leaves your network.
