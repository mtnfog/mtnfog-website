Mountain Fog provides software and consulting services around the intersections of cloud computing, big-data, and natural language processing (NLP). Our software to redact text and documents is used by customers worldwide in a variety of industries such as healthcare, insurance, financial, and legal. Our customers range from small businesses to large enterprises.

We welcome you to connect with us on LinkedIn and to follow us on Twitter. For technical support contact information please visit our support resources.

Partnerships and Affiliations

We partner with resellers and consulting companies to distribute our products. We place a high value on our partnerships because of the benefit to the customer and their level of satisfaction. We are always open to exploring new partnerships, and especially open to establishing new partnerships with AWS Consulting Partners for the AWS Marketplace Consulting Partner Private Offers (CPPO).
